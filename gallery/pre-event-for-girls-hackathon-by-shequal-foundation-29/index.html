Pre-event for Girls' Hackathon by Shequal Foundation

In an initiation of Research and Innovation Unit, RIU, and the Department of Electronics and Computer Engineering, of Advanced College of Engineering and Management, acem, a pre-event program was organized on 28th of December, 2022 in the premises of acem. This program was led by ‘Shequal Foundation’ where the mentors from ‘Skills Square’ were invited.
